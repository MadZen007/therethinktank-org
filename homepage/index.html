The Re-Think Tank Helping you find the answers you need...
by changing the questions you ask!

Have you been feeling it?
This feeling that we’re all so hopelessly divided. That we’re on different teams, living in different realities, fighting for completely opposite things.
It’s a lie.
A very convincing one, I’ll give you that. But a lie nonetheless.
When you strip away the noise, we ALL want the same things. We want to be safe. We want to be healthy. We want to build a better life for the people we care about. We all have fears. We all have obstacles. We all get that pit in our stomach when we think about the future.
The division we feel? That isn't what we want. It’s a symptom of how we’ve been taught to think. We’ve been sold this story that there’s only one right answer, and that anyone who disagrees isn't just wrong, they’re evil!
And so we refuse to find common ground anymore. We see compromise as weakness. We’d rather burn the whole house down than let the "other side" win a single battle.
We’ve been trained WHAT to think, instead of HOW to think.
Here, we aren’t interested in quick answers. We want to take the time to ask better questions.
What is the REAL goal here?
What are ALL of the options to get there?
Where is the most reliable source for the facts, and WHY do we trust them over the others?
What facts are we missing, or leaving out?
What are the pros and cons of EACH side?
And my favorite…..What if I am completely wrong?

By asking better questions, we can find all the flaws in our own answers, and see other possibilities more clearly. Shall we begin?
